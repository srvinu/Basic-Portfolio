Machine Learning (ML) is concerned about developing systems that improve their performance with experience. In the last decade progress in AI can easily be attributed to the advances in ML. ML is so popular that it has become synonymous with AI. The researchers are now focusing on scaling the state-of-the-art ML algorithms to large datasets.
